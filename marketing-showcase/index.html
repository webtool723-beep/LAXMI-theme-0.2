Ironclad Security & Access Control

In a fast-paced restaurant, data integrity is paramount. Our system is built on a strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) architecture.

Every login is verified. Every action is audited. No crossover, no confusion.

  • Waiters cannot access Admin settings.
  • Kitchen staff are locked to the Kitchen Dashboard.
  • Payment Managers cannot edit the core Menu.
  • Only the Super Admin holds the master keys.

Layer by Layer Excellence

Every person on your team has unique qualities. Our system provides them with dedicated tools tailored exactly to their function.

Super Admin

The ultimate overseer. Total control over the entire restaurant chain.

  • Absolute Access: Has access to every single feature enabled on the platform.
  • Chain Overview: Monitor multiple branches simultaneously.
  • Global Staffing: Add and manage staff across all locations.
  • Master Reports: Deep analytics and overriding administrative power.

Admin

The branch commander. Focused on optimizing a specific location.

  • Table & Room Management: Create and organize tables and VIP rooms.
  • Smart QR Generation: Generate specific QR codes for instant table ordering.
  • Order Oversight: View and manage all incoming order details in real-time.

Kitchen

The heart of the restaurant. Focused strictly on culinary execution.

  • Live Order Cards: Incoming orders appear instantly as distinct cards.
  • Waiter Assignment: Call specific waiters when a dish is ready for pickup.
  • Daily Summaries: Track total dishes prepared throughout the shift.
Intelligent Audio Alerts: Whenever a new order card appears on the kitchen dashboard, a high-fidelity bell sound alerts the chefs instantly. No missed orders, ever.

Menu Manager

The digital curator. Keeping the offerings fresh and accurate.

  • Category Control: Add, edit, or delete entire menu categories effortlessly.
  • Item Engineering: Update pricing, descriptions, and dietary tags.
  • Availability Toggles: Instantly mark items out-of-stock across the entire system.

Payment Manager

The financial hub. Secure, fast, and transparent transactions.

  • Direct Collections: Process digital and cash payments directly.
  • Waiter Dispatch: Send specific waiters to tables to collect cash.
  • Instant Receipts: Confirm payments and close out orders seamlessly.

Waiter

The frontline experience. Empowered with mobility and speed.

  • Mobile Dashboard: Take and review orders right at the table.
  • Table Transfers: Effortlessly transfer a guest's order to a different table or waiter.
  • Direct Kitchen Link: Fire orders directly to the kitchen with zero delay.

Customer Experience

The user portal (index.html). A frictionless dining journey.

  • QR & Location Selection: Scan to order dine-in, or use the interactive map for delivery.
  • Premium Notification Drawer: Guests receive live status updates right on their phones.
  • Cart & Checkout: Beautiful, intuitive browsing of categories and secure checkout.
